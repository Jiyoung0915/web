Cosmic Digital Incorporated

"Space Odyssey" is a game that our group "Cosmic Digital Incorporated" will be developing for our class CSC 4263. This game will feature a Run-And-Gun first person shooter experience set in a space. The players will have to fight their way though waves of hostile aliens inorder to survive.
